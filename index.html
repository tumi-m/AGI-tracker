Methodology

Transparent by construction

No secret sauce. The aggregate is a median anyone can recompute from the public dataset in about three lines of code.

Transparent median

The headline clock is the median of every tracked forecast date, recomputed automatically whenever any source updates. The dataset is a single public JSON file; edits happen via pull request, so every change carries an audit trail.

Sourced and verified

Dates are editorial interpretations of public statements and community medians — labeled as such, never words put in anyone's mouth. Every date links to its primary source and carries a last-verified stamp.

Revisions are content

When a forecaster moves — Kokotajlo's 2027 to 2030 revision was itself a news story — the change lands in a public changelog with the quote and the link. Expired forecasts stay on the board: history will judge.
